Intoduction

Singapore is a sunny, tropical island in Southeast Asia, off the southern tip of the Malay Peninsula.

The city-state is 710 square kilometres and inhabited by five million people from four major communities:-

  1. Chinese (majority)
  2. Malay
  3. Indian
  4. Eurasian

Cultures

Singapore contemporary modern culture consists of a combination of Asian and European cultures, mainly by Malay, South Asian, East Asian and Eurasian influences. Singapore has been dubbed as a country where "East meets West", "Gateway to Asia" and a "Garden city".

Many Singaporeans are bilingual. Most speak Singaporean English and another language, most commonly Mandarin, Malay, Tamil or Singapore Colloquial English (Singlish).

Peopele in this country are:-

  • Multi-racial
  • Multi-cultural
  • Multi-religious
  • Multi-lingual country

Environment

Singapore has a typical tropical climate with plenty of rainfall, consistent high temperatures, and high humidity all year round due to its proximity to the equator.

With some of the world's strangest and most varied wildlife, Singapore is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. Discover Singapore's natural treasures—beautiful beaches, cascading waterfalls, and exotic animals dwelling in verdant jungles—by reading on.

Singapore also know as:-

  1. Lion City

  3. First world country
